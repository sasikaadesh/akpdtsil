DealVision Analytics

DealVision Analytics is an advanced platform designed to give banks and financial institutions a clear, detailed view into the holdings of asset managers.

By aggregating 13F filings and other market data, DealVision enables users to easily identify who holds which assets, monitor changes in ownership, and dive deeper into sector focus, trends, and patterns. Built around a visually intuitive interface, DealVision makes it simple to answer key questions about investment strategies and market sentiments.
